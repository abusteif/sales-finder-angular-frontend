How do I create a price alert?

You can create a price alert in two convenient ways:

Method 1: From the Alerts Page

  1. Go to the Alerts page and click the "New Alert" button
  2. Enter a search text that we'll use to find items
  3. Select the store(s) you want to monitor for that item
  4. Choose your desired discount range and price range
  5. We'll notify you when matching items meet your criteria!

Method 2: From the Items Page

  1. Browse or search for products on the Items page
  2. Find the specific item you're interested in
  3. Click the bell icon on that item card
  4. This will create an alert for that specific item

You can manage all your alerts from the Alerts page in your account.

Why didn't I receive an email for an item that matched my alert criteria?

There are several reasons why you might not have received an alert email:

  • Check your junk/spam folder - Sometimes alert emails can be filtered as spam
  • Email forwarding issues - If you have email forwarding set up, the delivery to your final destination might have failed
  • Recent duplicate notification - You might have already received an email about the same item recently with a higher discount, and we avoid sending duplicate alerts within a short timeframe
  • Email delivery delays - There might be temporary delays with your email provider
  • Server maintenance - Occasionally, scheduled maintenance might temporarily affect email delivery

If you continue to experience issues, please check your email settings in your profile and consider adding our domain to your email whitelist. You can also contact us for further assistance.

Why do I see different discount amounts on your website compared to the retailer's website?

The discounts we show are the most up-to-date available. Sometimes even the retailer's website can take some time to update their prices, which can cause temporary discrepancies between what you see on our site and theirs.

Our system continuously monitors price changes and updates discount information in real-time. If you notice a significant difference, the retailer's website may still be showing cached or outdated pricing information.

What do the colors and symbols on the items mean?

Let's break it down:

  • Frame Color: Purple for new items, Green for discount increases, Red for discount decreases
  • 🔥 This item has the highest discount since we started tracking it
  • 30 How many days since this item had a higher discount
  • The discount has gone up since the last check
  • The discount has gone down since the last check
  • This item was on discount, went off discount, then came back on discount
  • This item is newly tracked
  • This item constantly goes up and down in price, which indicates a potentially misleading discount

These visual indicators help you quickly identify the best deals and understand price trends at a glance.

Tip: Hover over any icon on an item card to see additional details and tooltips with more specific information.
